2019 | Maturity Rating: 18+ | 3 Seasons | Sci-Fi

Terrifying creatures, wicked surprises and dark comedy converge in this NSFW anthology of animated stories presented by Tim Miller and David Fincher. Watch all you want. This series from Tim Miller and David Fincher won the 2019 and 2021 Emmys for Outstanding Short Form Animated Program.
